TaskFlow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TaskFlow. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with a functional task management experience, we collect the following:

  • Account Information: Your email address (provided via login).
  • User Content: The tasks, descriptions, and lists you create within the app.
  • Technical Data: Basic logs required for app stability and authentication via our service providers.

3. How Your Data is Used

We use your data solely to:

  • Maintain your account and sync your tasks across devices.
  • Provide essential app functionality (creating, editing, and deleting tasks).
  • Ensure the security and stability of the application.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a backend-as-a-service provider.

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Infrastructure: Data is hosted on secure servers managed by Supabase.
  • Access: Only you (the authenticated user) and the developer (for essential maintenance) have access to the data stored in the database.

5. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data.

  • Tasks: You can delete individual tasks at any time within the app UI.
  • Account Deletion: If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at sayera1900@gmail.com. We will process your request within 7 business days.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
